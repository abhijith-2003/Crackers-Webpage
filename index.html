Celebrate with Care

Simple steps for a safe, bright, and colorful celebration.

DO

  • Keep a fire extinguisher nearby
  • Use protective eyewear
  • Light one cracker at a time
  • Supervise children closely
  • Keep a bucket of water nearby

DON'T

  • Never light crackers indoors
  • Never hold crackers in your hand
  • Never relight a dud cracker
  • Never allow children to light crackers
  • Never light crackers under influence
